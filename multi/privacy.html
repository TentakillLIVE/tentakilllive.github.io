Privacy
Policy

Short version: we don't want your data, we don't collect your data.

The short version

We collect nothing.

EightEyes does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data. We have no servers receiving your information, no databases storing your activity, and no interest whatsoever in knowing who you are or how you use this site.

Your preferences

Everything stays on your machine.

Your layout preferences, stream tabs, and theme choice are saved using your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device — it is not transmitted to us or anyone else. You can clear it at any time through your browser settings.

Stored locally Theme, tabs, stream URLs and labels live in your browser's localStorage — on your computer, under your control.
Delete anytime Clear your browser's site data for this page and everything EightEyes has stored is gone immediately.
Analytics

We don't track usage.

There are no analytics scripts, no tracking pixels, no session recording tools, and no third-party monitoring services embedded in EightEyes. We genuinely do not care how many people use this site, where they come from, or what they do with it.

Third-party embeds

Twitch and YouTube have their own policies.

EightEyes embeds streams from Twitch and YouTube using their official embed players. Once those players load, you are subject to their respective privacy policies. We have no control over what Twitch or YouTube collect within their embeds, and that data never passes through us.

The extension

The Turbo Helper extension is also fully local.

The optional EightEyes Turbo Helper browser extension reads your Twitch authentication token directly from your browser's cookie store. That token is only ever used to authenticate requests going directly to Twitch's own servers — it is never sent to us, logged, or stored anywhere outside your browser.

The bottom line We built EightEyes to watch streams, not to harvest data. Your privacy is not a feature — it's simply a consequence of how this site is built.
